<p><p>The Reverse Osmosis (RO) membrane market is primarily categorized into composite membranes and asymmetric membranes. Composite membranes consist of multiple layers, offering enhanced performance and selectivity, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, including desalination and wastewater treatment. Asymmetric membranes have a varying thickness, featuring a porous support layer topped with a thin selective layer, optimizing filtration efficiency. Both types cater to different needs in water purification, chemical processing, and industrial applications, contributing to improved water quality and sustainability.</p></p>
