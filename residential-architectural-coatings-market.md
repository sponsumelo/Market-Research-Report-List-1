<p><p>The residential architectural coatings market includes several key types. Water-soluble coatings are environmentally friendly and easy to clean, ideal for indoor use. Solvent coatings offer durability and resistance to weather, suitable for exterior applications but may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Emulsion coatings combine water-based and additive properties, providing good adhesion and flexibility, making them popular for various surfaces. Each type caters to specific needs in aesthetics, protection, and environmental considerations within residential settings.</p></p>
