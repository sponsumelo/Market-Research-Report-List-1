<p><strong>The Edge Routers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Subscriber Edge Router</li><li>Label Edge Router</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Edge routers play a crucial role in network architecture by connecting end-users to service provider networks. The Subscriber Edge Router primarily manages traffic entering the service provider's network from subscribers, ensuring efficient data routing and access management. In contrast, the Label Edge Router (LER) is integral to MPLS (Multiprotocol Label Switching) networks, handling label-based routing for data packets to optimize traffic flow and improve performance. Together, these routers enhance network efficiency and user experience in diverse applications.</p></p>
