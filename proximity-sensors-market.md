<p><p>Proximity sensors are vital in detecting the presence or absence of objects without direct contact. Inductive sensors work with metallic objects, while capacitive sensors detect both metallic and non-metallic targets. LVDT (Linear Variable Differential Transformer) sensors measure linear displacement with high precision. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to identify object distance, suitable for various materials. Photoelectric sensors utilize light beams for presence detection, effective in diverse applications. Together, these sensors enhance automation and improve efficiency across industries.</p></p>
