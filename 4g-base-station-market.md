<p><p>The 4G base station market comprises two primary types: macrocell and small cell networks. Macrocell stations cover large areas, providing extensive connectivity and supporting high user capacities, ideal for urban and suburban environments. In contrast, small cells focus on enhancing coverage and capacity in dense urban areas, often placed on street furniture or buildings. They complement macrocell networks, addressing capacity demands and improving user experience by providing localized coverage in hotspots and challenging areas.</p></p>
