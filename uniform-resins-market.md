<p><p>The Uniform Resins market encompasses two primary types: cation resins and anion resins. Cation resins are used to remove positively charged ions from solutions, facilitating water softening and purification. In contrast, anion resins target negatively charged ions, playing a vital role in deionization and wastewater treatment. Both types are essential in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and power generation, by ensuring high-quality water and effective ion exchange processes for improved product and process efficiencies.</p></p>
