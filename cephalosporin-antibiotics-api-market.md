<p><p>The Cephalosporin Antibiotics API market is segmented into four generations, each offering distinct characteristics. First generation cephalosporins primarily target gram-positive bacteria, making them effective for skin infections. Second generation cephalosporins expand coverage to some gram-negative bacteria. Third generation cephalosporins provide broader spectrum activity, including serious infections caused by resistant strains. Fourth generation cephalosporins enhance stability against beta-lactamases and further target resistant bacteria, making them ideal for hospital settings. Each generation reflects advancements in antibiotic efficacy and spectrum of activity.</p></p>
