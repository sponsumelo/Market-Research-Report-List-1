<p><p>The multi-axial fabric market encompasses various types of fabrics designed for composite materials, enhancing their strength and durability. Uniaxial fabric features fibers oriented in a single direction, providing high strength along that axis. Biaxial fabric has fibers woven at two angles, improving performance in multiple directions. Triaxial fabric incorporates three orientations, offering enhanced stability and resistance. Quadaxial fabric layers fibers in four directions for maximum structural integrity. Other variations include specialized fabrics for niche applications, catering to diverse industries such as aerospace and automotive.</p></p>
