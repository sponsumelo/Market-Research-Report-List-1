<p><p>Microencapsulated Paraffin Phase Change Materials (PCMs) are advanced thermal energy storage solutions where paraffin wax, a phase change material, is encapsulated in microscopic shells. These materials absorb, store, and release thermal energy during phase transitions, making them highly effective for temperature regulation in various applications, including building insulation, textiles, electronics, and cold chain logistics. The encapsulation process enhances durability, prevents leakage, and improves thermal stability, making them versatile for industrial and commercial use.</p><p>The Microencapsulated Paraffin Phase Change Materials Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.2% during the forecast period. Additionally, advancements in encapsulation technologies and the growing use of PCMs in textiles for thermal comfort are contributing to market expansion.</p><p>Latest trends include the development of bio-based paraffin PCMs to enhance sustainability, integration of PCMs in smart textiles, and their application in renewable energy systems.
