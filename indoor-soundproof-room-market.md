<p><p>The indoor soundproof room market is divided into two main types: single wall and double wall structures. Single wall soundproof rooms use a single layer of acoustic materials to minimize noise transmission, often suitable for smaller applications or budget-conscious consumers. In contrast, double wall soundproof rooms feature two layers of walls separated by an air gap, significantly enhancing sound insulation and making them ideal for professional or high-performance environments. Each type caters to different needs regarding soundproofing efficiency and cost.</p></p>
