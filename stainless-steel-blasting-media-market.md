<p><p>The stainless steel blasting media market comprises two main types: stainless steel shot and stainless steel grit. Stainless steel shot is spherical, providing uniform surface finishing and is ideal for delicate applications like cleaning and polishing. In contrast, stainless steel grit features angular particles, which are more aggressive and suitable for heavier tasks such as surface preparation and rust removal. Both types offer durability and resistance to corrosion, making them preferred options in various industrial applications.</p></p>
