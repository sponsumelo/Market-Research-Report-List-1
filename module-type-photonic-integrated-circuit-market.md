<p><strong>The Module Type Photonic Integrated Circuit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Photonic Integrated Circuit</li><li>Active Photonic Integrated Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photonic integrated circuit (PIC) market is divided into two main types: passive and active photonic integrated circuits. Passive PICs do not require an external power source to function and include components like waveguides, filters, and multiplexers, primarily used for signal routing. In contrast, active PICs integrate components like lasers, detectors, and modulators that require power to operate, enabling dynamic functions like signal generation and amplification. Both types are essential for applications in telecommunications, data centers, and optical networks.</p></p>
