<p><p>The sulfuric acid market is categorized by its production sources. **Elemental Sulfur** involves burning sulfur to produce sulfur dioxide, which is then converted to sulfuric acid, commonly used in fertilizers and chemicals. **Base Metal Smelters** generate sulfuric acid as a byproduct during the extraction of metals like copper and zinc. **Pyrite Ore** involves roasting iron pyrite to produce sulfur dioxide, which is processed into sulfuric acid. **Others** include alternative sources like spent acid regeneration and non-ferrous metal processing. Each type caters to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
