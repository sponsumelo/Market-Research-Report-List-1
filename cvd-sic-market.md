<p><ul><li>High Resistivity Grade</li><li>Middle Resistivity Grade</li><li>Low Resistivity Grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CVD SiC (Chemical Vapor Deposition Silicon Carbide) markets are categorized into three resistivity grades: high, middle, and low. High resistivity grade SiC is used in applications requiring excellent insulation and minimal conductivity, such as in high-voltage devices. Middle resistivity grade SiC balances conductivity and resistivity, making it suitable for general-purpose electronics. Low resistivity grade SiC offers high conductivity, ideal for power electronic devices and high-frequency applications, catering to the growing demand in sectors like automotive and telecommunications.</p></p>
