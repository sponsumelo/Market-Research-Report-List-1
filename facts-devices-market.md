Meanwhile, the Static Var Generator (SVG) market involves advanced devices that dynamically adjust reactive power to respond to varying load conditions, offering improved performance and reduced electrical losses. Both types are essential for optimizing grid reliability and efficiency in modern electricity networks.</p></p>
