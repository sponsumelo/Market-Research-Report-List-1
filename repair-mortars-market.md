<p><p>The repair mortars market is primarily categorized into two main types: Polymer-Modified Cementitious Mortar and Epoxy-Based Mortar. Polymer-Modified Cementitious Mortar incorporates polymers to enhance flexibility, adhesion, and durability, making it suitable for various repair applications in concrete structures. In contrast, Epoxy-Based Mortar offers superior chemical resistance and bonding capabilities, ideal for high-stress environments. Both types are crucial for maintaining structural integrity, ensuring long-lasting repairs, and improving the overall performance of construction projects.</p></p>
